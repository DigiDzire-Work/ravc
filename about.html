RAVC as an idea was conceptualized way back in 2019 by Mr. Ram Anuj Verma and his college mates. It was formally incorporated in 2021 as a Private Limited Company providing Solar Power Plant Services.

Within a short span of time, RAVC Solutions Pvt Ltd has established a healthy clientele base and expanded its business portfolio providing services like Utility Scale Solar Projects Engineering Procurement & Construction (EPC) Services, Solar Plant Operation & Maintenance (O&M) services, Solar Project Management Consultancy (PMC) services, Rooftop Solar [Residential, Commercial & Industrial(C&I)] turnkey Solution and Independent Power Producer (IPP)/ Solar Power Generator (SPG).

Further, we have plans to expand into the domains like Compressed Biogas (CBG /Bio-CNG) Generation and 3rd Party Open Access (OA) Projects in near future.

At RAVC, we are driven by our vision to become a leader in the Renewable Energy industry. We strive to consistently exceed client expectations, deliver sustainable and efficient solutions, and contribute to the growth and development of the renewable energy sector in India.

Our Journey

Since our inception, our commitment has been to provide our clients with the best services and solutions. Utmost adherence to quality and respective cost-effectiveness has been integral to our journey so far. Our unwavering dedication to continuous improvement has allowed us to enhance plant efficiency while reducing operational costs through process optimization techniques.

Maintaining the highest standards of quality while ensuring cost-effectiveness has been a fundamental aspect of our identity. Furthermore, we prioritize capacity building, fostering a learning environment, and attracting and nurturing the finest talent in the industry.

RAVC Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is an emerging quality service provider, headquartered in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.
